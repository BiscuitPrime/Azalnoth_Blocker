Azalnoth Blocker

An app developed by the Inner Eye Ministry per Article 2235-89.

According to Article 2235-89, "Applications, sites or any software accessible through standard and non-standard means on physical or non-physical devices, that have a main or secondary stated or unstated goal to squander a user's time, willing or unwilling, through the willfull or unwillfull use of addiction tactics such as listed in the following sections, shall see their access be prevented".


This application serves as the enforcement of this article in accordance with Article 189-1/A in regards with interference from the Sanctuary of Watchful Gaze.
